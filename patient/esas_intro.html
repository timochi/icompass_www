Edmonton Symptom Assessment System (ESAS)


This tool is designed to assist in the assessment of nine symptoms common in cancer patients:



Symptom Description
Pain Any 'discomfort'
Tiredness Lack of energy
Nausea Upset stomach, feeling like you could 'throw up'
Depression Feelings of sadness and hopelessness
Anxiety Feelings of worry, nervousness, or unease
Drowsiness Feeling sleepy
Shortness of breath Difficulty breathing
Appetite Wanting to eat
Well-being Overall comfort, both physical and otherwise
